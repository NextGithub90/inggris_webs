Learn the difference between common mispronunciation and standard English pronunciation through audio and IPA comparisons.

Many English learners unknowingly repeat pronunciation mistakes because they hear them frequently in everyday life. This website helps you recognize those mistakes, compare them with standard pronunciation, and improve your pronunciation skill in a pressure-free learning environment.

Why Pronunciation Matters?

Clear Communication

Good pronunciation helps people understand you easily. Wrong pronunciation can change the meaning completely.

Build Confidence

Pronouncing words correctly makes you feel more confident and less afraid of making mistakes.

Avoid Fossilization

Repeated mistakes can become habits if they are never recognized.

Creates Impression

Clear pronunciation makes you sound more fluent and professional, especially in academic or work situations.
